Filipino Chicken Adobo

This is a Tasty Filipino Chicken Adobo we will be making!

Adobo is Filipino dish that is commonly serve in every Filipino household, regardless if there's an event or not, this is the crowds favorite. There are a lot of variations of an Adobo, it can be chicken, pork or even fish! We're just gonna focus on cooking chicken for now since it's my favorite!

First, you're gonna need the following ingredients to start cooking!

  • 4 to 5 pounds chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 3 bay leaves
  • Steamed rice, for serving

Now let's get cooking once you got everything ready!

  1. Combine the chicken thighs, vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, peppercorns and bay leaves in a large pot. Cover and marinate the chicken in the refrigerator for 1 to 3 hours.
  2. Bring the chicken to a boil over high heat. Lower the heat, cover and let simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove the lid and simmer until the sauce is reduced and thickened and the chicken is tender, about 20 more minutes. Serve with steamed rice.

That's it! Enjoy your meal and don't forget to eat a whole lot of rice when eating Adobo!
